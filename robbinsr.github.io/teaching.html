Teaching

I have been fortunate to be able to teach a wide range of students. Kinds of students I have taught include working professional graduate students, working professional certificate students, working professional undergraduate students, as well as "traditional" graduate and undergraduate students.

I have taught in the classroom, in "flipped" classrooms, and online. Flipped classrooms are situations where the classroom is focused on students doing to learn, as opposed to passively listening to lectures.

I am a firm believer in self-reflection. In this vein I have consistently researched how to teach and subsequently applied my findings. A paper about my teaching and a workshop for my students are below. Evaluations of my learning-about-teaching efforts are also below.
